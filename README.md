## Who Is FEB CLOUD?

FEB CLOUD (FEB PTE. LTD.) is a Singapore-registered cloud services company headquartered at Paya Lebar Square. They run their own Singapore Tier III data center and offer a full stack of internet services: shared hosting, VPS cloud infrastructure, professional email, VPN, SSL certificates, domain registration, and custom web development.

What stands out is that they're not just a reseller — they operate their own cloud platform (FEB CLOUD CORE), which gives them the flexibility to price cloud VPS configurations at around 30% below AWS and Google Cloud equivalents.

The team supports both English and Chinese, which matters a lot for businesses operating across Southeast Asia and mainland China.

### FEB CLOUD CORE — Enterprise Cloud Infrastructure

This is the VPS/cloud platform for developers and technical teams. Think AWS but smaller, focused on Asia, and cheaper. They cover cloud hosting, databases, object storage, CDN, DDoS protection, container services, and more. Billing is straightforward — hourly or monthly, with the full monthly estimate shown at checkout.

### 5UF VPN — Proven China Connectivity

15+ years of operation, specifically optimized for users in mainland China. Works on iOS, Android, Windows, macOS, and as a Chrome extension. The annual plan works out to 12.50 SGD/month (billed as 150 SGD/year). There's a 7-day no-questions-asked refund policy.

### Professional Email (OX App Suite)

Powered by OX App Suite, this is a proper business email platform with 10GB–50GB mailboxes, shared calendaring, cloud file storage, and compatibility with Microsoft Office documents. Works with all IMAP clients and syncs across devices.
